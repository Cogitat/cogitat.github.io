Mission

Cogitat's mission is to make implementing and deploying Deep Learning easy for enterprises.

What We Do

Cogitat applies Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence to the most difficult problems in data analytics. We design, develop, and deploy state-of-the-art, scalable deep learning solutions for enterprises. Deep learning is becoming an important tool set for image and audio regonition, natural-language processing, pattern recognition, and fraud detection.

We build on top of open-source frameworks TensorFlow and CNTK and have expertise in building and deploying cloud-based systems on Amazon AWS and Google Compute Engine. Our mission goal is to make deploying Deep Learning easy for enterprises.
